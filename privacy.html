Lendmate Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

Lendmate ("we", "our", or "us") is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and UPI ID.
  • Transaction Data: We collect details about the items and funds you lend or borrow, including amounts, due dates, and descriptions.
  • Communication Data: We collect messages sent through our in-app chat feature to facilitate peer-to-peer lending.
  • Media: Photos of items or transaction proofs you upload are stored using Cloudinary.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To facilitate lending and borrowing between you and your contacts.
  • To send push notifications regarding loan requests and due dates.
  • To provide customer support and improve the app experience.

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

Your data is securely stored using Firebase (Authentication and Firestore) and Cloudinary. While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. SHARING YOUR INFORMATION

Your personal and transaction data are only visible to the specific users (friends) you interact with inside the app. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

6. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time through the app settings.

7. CONTACT US

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's support channel.
